Valorant

Valorant, a team-based tactical shooter by Riot Games, combines elements of games like Counter-Strike with character abilities reminiscent of Apex Legends and Overwatch. Matches involve two teams of five players facing off on various maps, each with unique layouts. One team attacks by planting a bomb, while the other defends. Teams switch sides every 12 rounds, aiming to win by securing 13 rounds, with a two-round advantage required in competitive mode. Additional modes like free-for-all deathmatch offer practice opportunities with different weapons.

League of Legends

League of Legends (LoL) by Riot Games is a highly popular multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) known for its strategic depth and diverse champions. Two teams of five players compete to destroy the enemy's Nexus while defending their own. Players select champions with unique abilities, aiming for victory through teamwork and skillful tactics. Matches unfold on maps like Summoner's Rift, where players earn gold and experience to enhance their champions' power. The game's rich lore adds depth to its characters and world.
